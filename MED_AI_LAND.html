// Triage System

Three levels.
One clear answer.

MedAI classifies every symptom report into one of three urgent action levels — no guessing, no medical jargon, just a clear next step.

Level 01
🏠
Stay Home
Manageable symptoms. MedAI gives you specific home care steps, what to monitor, and when to reassess — so you don't waste a hospital trip.
Level 02
🏥
See a Doctor
Needs professional attention within 24–48 hours. Not an emergency — but don't ignore it. MedAI explains exactly why and what to tell your doctor.
Level 03
🚨
Go Now
Possible life-threatening condition detected. MedAI flags every red flag and tells you to seek emergency care immediately — no hesitation.

// Transparency

Honest about
what it is.

🤖

AI Guidance, Not Diagnosis

MedAI provides intelligent triage guidance and preliminary assessment based on your reported symptoms and health indicators. It is not a replacement for a licensed medical professional and should never be used as the sole basis for medical decisions. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

🔒

Your Data Stays Private

Symptom descriptions are processed in real-time and never stored. Nothing you share is saved, logged, or sold.

⚠️

Always Call Emergency Services

In a life-threatening emergency, call your local emergency number immediately — do not wait for an AI assessment.
