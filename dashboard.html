// FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Is MedAI a replacement for a real doctor?▾

No. MedAI provides AI-powered triage guidance only. It helps you understand the urgency of your symptoms, but is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment.

Is my health data stored or shared?▾

Your profile, triage history, vitals, reminders, and chat history are stored locally in this browser for now. Backend storage and account sync can be added later.

How accurate is the triage system?▾

MedAI's AI model achieves approximately 94% accuracy in triage classification based on internal testing. Each result includes a confidence score so you can gauge reliability.

What is the Premium plan?▾

Premium unlocks unlimited triage sessions, advanced AI consultations, detailed health reports, priority support, and early access to new features.

How do I add emergency contacts?▾

Go to Settings → Emergency Contacts. You can add up to 5 contacts with their name and phone number for quick dial access during an emergency.
